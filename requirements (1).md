# Vyasa AI - Requirements

## Problem Statement

Rural and underserved communities across India struggle to access government schemes, education, jobs, and welfare services due to:
- **Language barriers** and low literacy
- **Poor internet connectivity** 
- **Complex application processes**
- **Dependence on middlemen**

Despite available services, communities remain unaware and excluded.

## Core Design Principles

- **Inclusion First:** For rural, elderly, women, low-literacy users
- **Voice-First:** Speech interaction over typing
- **Local Languages:** Telugu, Hindi, Tamil + regional languages
- **Offline-Friendly:** Works on 2G/3G or no internet
- **Action-Oriented:** Focus on "what to do next"

## Key Features

### 1. Voice-First Interaction
- Natural speech in regional languages
- Example: "What schemes are available for women?"
- Simplified explanations for low-literacy users

### 2. Personalized Recommendations
- Minimal context (age, occupation, location)
- Eligibility-based filtering
- Step-by-step guidance with required documents

### 3. Explainable AI (Trust-Building)
- Clear reasoning: "This scheme is for you because you are a small farmer from Andhra Pradesh"
- Transparent eligibility criteria

### 4. Offline-First Intelligence
- Pre-downloaded knowledge packs (government schemes, eligibility rules, documents)
- State-specific and language-specific content
- Works without internet, syncs when available

### 5. Community Kiosk Mode
- Shared access in panchayat offices, schools, health centers
- No login required, privacy-safe sessions
- Enables access for non-smartphone users

### 6. Multi-Channel Access
- Mobile app, WhatsApp bot, IVR phone system
- Maximum reach across user capabilities

### 7. Feedback & Analytics
- Simple "Helpful/Not Helpful" feedback
- Insights for authorities: most asked questions, awareness gaps

## Target Users

- Rural citizens, farmers, daily workers
- Women, elderly, low-literacy users
- Students and job seekers
- Non-smartphone users

## Success Metrics

- **10,000+ users** within 6 months
- **85% satisfaction** rate
- **70% query success** rate
- **50% increase** in scheme awareness
- **30% reduction** in middleman dependency

## Technical Requirements

- Works on low-end Android devices (1GB RAM)
- <3 seconds response time
- 99.5% uptime for offline functionality
- Support 10+ languages, 5+ states
- No personal data storage (privacy-first)
